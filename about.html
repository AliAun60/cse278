Application Overview:

Objective: The application aims to facilitate the management of customer orders by providing a user-friendly interface to query and display relevant information.
It connects to a MySQL database and executes SQL queries to retrieve and present data related to customers, orders, payments, and products.
The primary goal is to enable users to inquire about specific attributes related to customer orders.

Table Used And Queries:

  • Offices
  • Employees
  • Customers
  • Payments
  • Orders
  • OrderDetails
  • Products

  • Order Inquire Details Query:
    Description: Generates the summary of the order Date and Order Number and their status along with their name and full address.

  • Product And Payment Query:
    Description: Retrieve a list of customers who have paid the Amount for the orders placed, along with their Customer Number and Name, Payment Amount and Date, and the product Bought.
